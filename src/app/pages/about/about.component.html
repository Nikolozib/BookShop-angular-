Our Story

A passion for books, turned into a place for everyone

Born From a Love of Reading

PageTurn started in 2018 as a small shelf of handpicked books in a San Francisco coffee shop. What began as a weekend hobby quickly became something much bigger — a community of readers who believe the right book at the right moment can genuinely change a life.

Today we stock over 5,000 titles across every genre imaginable. Our team reads every book we carry and writes honest, personal notes about why it deserves a place on your shelf.

We believe bookshops should feel warm, welcoming, and a little bit magical — and we have tried to bring that spirit to every corner of our online store.

What We Stand For

The values that guide everything we do

Sustainability

We partner with publishers who use recycled paper and offset their carbon footprint. Our packaging is 100% recyclable.

Community

Books bring people together. We run monthly reading groups, author events, and donate a portion of every sale to literacy charities.

Curation

Every title on our shelves has been read and recommended by a real human. No algorithm, no filler — just books we genuinely love.

Accessibility

Great books should be affordable. We offer regular sales, a student discount programme, and free shipping on orders over $35.
