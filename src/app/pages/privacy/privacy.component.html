Privacy Policy

How PageTurn collects, uses, and protects your personal information

Last updated: May 2026. We may update this page from time to time; the version on our website is always the one that applies.

Who we are

PageTurn (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the PageTurn website and related services. When you shop with us or create an account, this policy explains what data we handle and why.

Information we collect

When you browse or shop

We collect information you give us directly — for example your name, email address, delivery address, and payment details processed by our payment partners. We also receive technical data such as browser type, device information, and approximate location to help keep the site secure and improve your experience.

When you contact us

If you email us or use a contact form, we keep your message and contact details so we can respond and follow up on any order-related questions.

Accounts

If you register an account, we store the credentials and profile information you provide so you can sign in, save preferences, and (where available) sync your cart across devices.

How we use your information

  • To process and deliver your orders, and to send order updates by email or SMS where you have opted in.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues, and to comply with legal obligations.
  • To improve our website, product range, and customer support — including anonymised analytics where we use third-party tools.
  • To send marketing communications only if you have agreed to receive them; you can unsubscribe at any time.

Legal bases (where applicable)

Depending on your region, we rely on one or more of: performing a contract with you, our legitimate interests (such as improving our services and security), your consent, or legal requirements.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with service providers who help us run our business — for example payment processors, shipping carriers, email delivery services, and hosting or analytics providers — and only to the extent they need it to perform those services on our behalf.

We may disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of PageTurn, our customers, or others.

Retention

We keep order and account records for as long as needed to fulfil our obligations to you, meet accounting and legal requirements, and resolve disputes. Marketing preferences and unsubscribe records are kept so we can honour your choices.

Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure; we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, or to object to or restrict certain processing. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at JonDoe@gmail.com and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies where necessary for the site to function, and where allowed for analytics or preferences. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may limit certain features.

Children

Our services are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe we have done so in error, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

International transfers

If you access our services from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where we or our providers operate, under safeguards required by applicable law.

Questions

If anything here is unclear, or you would like a copy of this policy in another format, we would be glad to help.
