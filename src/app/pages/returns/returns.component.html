Returns & Refunds

We want you to love every book you order — here is how we make things right when something is not quite perfect

These guidelines apply to orders placed through PageTurn. If you have a special circumstance, reach out and we will do our best to help.

Delivery & shipping

We dispatch most orders within one business day. Standard delivery times depend on your carrier and location; you will receive tracking details by email as soon as your parcel leaves our warehouse.

Free shipping applies to orders over $35 after discounts, shipped to addresses within the United States unless otherwise stated at checkout.

Our promise

If your book arrives damaged, incorrect, or not as described, we will replace it or refund you in full — including reasonable return postage where we are at fault.

30-day returns

For any other reason, you may return most new, unread books within 30 days of delivery for a full refund of the item price. The book should be in resalable condition, with any shrink-wrap or seals intact where applicable.

  • Personalised or made-to-order items cannot be returned unless faulty.
  • Digital products and gift cards are non-refundable once delivered, except where required by law.

How to start a return

  1. Email us at JonDoe@gmail.com with your order number and the reason for the return.
  2. We will send you a return authorisation and the address to send your parcel.
  3. Pack the book securely (original packaging helps) and post it back using a tracked service where possible.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5 business days. Refunds are made to your original payment method. Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take a few additional days to appear on your statement.

Exchanges

Need a different edition or title? The quickest path is usually to place a new order for the item you want and return the original book for a refund under the policy above — just mention both orders in your email so we can link them.
